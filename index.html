Overview

The Crypto-Anarchist Manifesto declares that as technology improves the world head towards crypto-anarchy. In other words crypto graphics will be so precise that interactions and transactions that happen on the internet will be untrace able. This will mean:

  • People will be able to exchange messages, conduct business, and negotiate electronic contracts without ever knowing the legal identity of another person and keeping tehir identity a secret.
  • Government regulation will be completely alterred. Taxation and controlling the economy will be difficult to do.
  • The ability to keep information secret and even the nature of trust itself will be altered

Problems and obstacles

The governement will try to halt this technology citing the following reasons:

These concerns are valid.Criminals and terrorists will unfortunately take advantage of the Crypto net. However these concerns will not halt the rise of crypto-anarchism

Conclusion

Crypto anarchism will revolutionize the social landscape the same way the printing press destroyed the power of guilds in medeival society, Crypto anarchism will destroy the power that corporations and the government has on the economy. A liquid market for anything that can be turned into words or pictures will be created and this market will alter the very nature of intellectual property
